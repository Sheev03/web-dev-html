Robert Burns Birthplace Museum

Visit the birthplace of Scotland’s national poet and a celebration of his enduring legacy.
Burns museum offers a fantastic tour of the Burns cottage and gives an insight into life centuries ago. The museum holds more than 5,000 artefacts belonging to or related to the poet. And once you have finished the tour of the museum, you can enjoy traditional Scottish meals in the café, such as haggis, neeps and tatties.
There are many attractions at the Robert Burns bithplace museum including:
  • Exhibitions on the life of Burns
  • Artwork
  • Brig o’Doon
  • Garden and Playpark
  • Tours of Burns Cottage
  • Giftshop

Preswick Golf Club

Rich in character, the famous clubhouse is as much part of the Ayr experience as the celebrated course over which the Open Championship was first played. The history of Prestwick Golf Club stretches back over 160 years to a time when golf was in its infancy. In 1851, a group of 57 enthusiastic members, made the decision to form a golf club purchasing two cottages opposite the tavern. There are many things to enjoy at Preswick Golf Club including:
  • Golf Course
  • Smokeroom
  • Restaurant
  • Dining Room
