South Ayrshire


The town of Ayr is a popular tourist resort, just southwest of the city of Glasgow. It comes complete with its stunning beach and great views of the Isle of Arran.
Furthermore, there are plenty of activities in Ayr such as are many golf courses or our Racecourse which dates all the way back to the 16th century and has been the chosen venue for many Scottish Grand Nationals and Ayr Gold Cup.
One of Scotland’s most influential figures, Robert Burns, was born here in Ayr, anyone wishing to take a visit to the Burns cottage can find out more in our Must Visit webpage.
There are also great shopping and dining facilities in Ayr.
